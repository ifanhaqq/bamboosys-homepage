Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BambooCloud's features, pricing, and implementation.

General Information

What is BambooCloud?
BambooCloud is an enterprise-grade Learning Management System (LMS) designed to create, deliver, and track online training. It serves K-12 education, higher education institutions, and corporate enterprises with a unified, cloud-native platform.
Is BambooCloud cloud-based or on-premise?
We are primarily a cloud-native SaaS solution (Software as a Service) hosted on secure global infrastructure. However, for government or highly regulated industries, we offer private cloud and hybrid deployment options to meet data sovereignty requirements.

Features & Functionality

Does BambooCloud support SCORM?
Yes! BambooCloud is fully compliant with SCORM 1.2, SCORM 2004, and xAPI (Tin Can). You can easily import existing courseware from tools like Articulate Storyline or Adobe Captivate directly into the platform.
Is there a mobile app available?
Absolutely. We provide native mobile applications for both iOS and Android. The apps support offline learning, allowing students to download content and sync their progress once they are back online.
Can I customize the look and feel of my portal?
Yes, BambooCloud supports extensive white-labeling. You can configure your own domain URL, upload your logo, change the color scheme to match your brand identity, and even customize the login page background.

Technical & Support

How secure is my data?
Security is our top priority. We are ISO 27001 certified and use industry-standard encryption for data at rest and in transit. We also perform regular penetration testing and vulnerability assessments.
Do you support Single Sign-On (SSO)?
Yes, we support major SSO protocols including SAML 2.0, OAuth 2.0, and OpenID Connect. We integrate seamlessly with Microsoft Azure AD, Okta, Google Workspace, and other identity providers.
