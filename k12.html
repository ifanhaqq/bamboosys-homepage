BambooCloud K12 Smart Learning

BambooCloud K12 smart learning platform is the next generation learning solution based on the latest cloud and AI technology. Aiming to create the future-ready learning space in the digital age, helping each student to unleash their full potential.

Our platform is precise in identifying areas to increase student engagement which leads to significant improvements in results. By combining data analytics with intuitive interfaces, we bridge the gap between teachers, students, and parents.

Knowledge Radar Chart

Students will acknowledge the detailed progress and performance of each knowledge point in a particular course. Visualize strengths and weaknesses instantly to achieve targeted learning paths.

Smart Error Bank

The system automatically generates a personalized "Error Bank" for each student after answering questions. Students can review specific mistakes, filtering by knowledge point, difficulty level, or question type.

Customized Learning Report

Generate comprehensive reports that go beyond grades. Teachers and parents get deep insights into learning behaviors, time management, and concept mastery to support the student's journey.

Self Test Support

Empower student agency. Students can create their own self-tests to target weak areas, selecting specific chapters or difficulty levels to practice at their own pace.
